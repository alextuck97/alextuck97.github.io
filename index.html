StockWatch

StockWatch is an application to make observing trends in the stock market more accessible to all.

User's can browse real time stock information scraped from the NYSE. They can add stocks they are interested in to a list of stocks to track further. In their portfolio users can investigate historical trends of the stocks they've chosen.
